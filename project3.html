Project 3: Vogue Project - Intimate Bottle Design

This project, titled "Vogue Project," centers on creating an intimate bottle design for females, accompanied by a complete packaging solution. The concept focuses on elegance and personal connection, crafting a product that resonates with femininity and sophistication. The design features clean, minimalistic elements with an emphasis on tactile experiences, creating a product that feels both luxurious and personal.
