Project 2: Doces Coloridos Branding

A vibrant and playful brand identity was created for "Doces Coloridos," a brand specializing in colorful sweets for children's parties. The goal was to craft a cohesive visual identity that resonates with both kids and parents, while highlighting the brand's mission of making its treats easily transportable. The design emphasizes portability and adaptability, ensuring the sweets can bring joy and fun to any celebration, wherever it takes place.
